Trivia game rules

Welcome to the best trivia game in the world - TriKi2! Test your knowledge and aim for the top of the leaderboard. Here’s how the game works:

1. Scoring system

Your score is based on the difficulty level you choose and how quickly and accurately you answer the questions.
Points per correct answer:
Easy: 10 points
Medium: 20 points
Hard: 30 points

2. Time-up penalty

You have 20 seconds to answer each question. If you don’t answer a question within the 20-second timer, the system will mark your answer as incorrect, also show you the correct answer, and notify you with the message: "Time is up!".

3. Streak bonuses

Consistency pays off! Earn bonus points by getting consecutive answers right:
3 correct answers in a row: points for those 3 questions are doubled.
5 correct answers in a row: points for those 5 questions are tripled.

4. Leaderboard

Compete with others and see where you stand on the leaderboard. Your score reflects not only how many questions you answer correctly but also how consistently you can keep your streaks going. Good luck and have fun testing your knowledge!
